THE DESIGNER

My name is Jason Decker Sloves.

My favorite color is red, yellow and blue are good too.

I really like the number 3.

When I was 12 my favorite color was green... I don't know how I remember that.

I like to make work that makes people really uncomfortable or confused. Example: my advanced typography final (video) on the "Fifty Shades" trilogy.

I've wanted to work in publishing (GQ, Architectural Digest) doing layout design, since ninth grade.

Since 2016, I've filled 5 Moleskine notebooks. I don't really sketch but I call them my "sketchbooks."

I love bomber jackets.

Tiramisu is my favorite food.

When I was 5, my walker was medium grey-light cobalt blue with black handles. I would cover it in stickers. It was so ugly.

Noe Text & Noe Display (Schick Toikka) are two of my favorite font families because I really like the sharp, triangle serifs.
