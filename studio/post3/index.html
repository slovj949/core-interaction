003—Red Part 2

Besides being the associated with energy, dominance, passion and danger, red has always been the color of live and love. Symbolically, red can be linked to romance. For example, red roses on Valentine’s day…

Variations of red include crimson and maroon. The connotations of the colors always very. Darker colors represent dominance and sophistication, while the lighter shades of red represent energy and are a little bit more playful…

When designing it good to use red sparingly. Red has a lot of positive and negative traits and it’ll often overwhelm the user.
