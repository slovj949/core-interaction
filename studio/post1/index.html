Post 1—Study Shows Red Plates Make You Eat Less

According to the an article on LA Weekly, by Benjamin Caro, researchers have found that people eating off of red plates tend to eat less compared to eating off any other colored plate. Although plate science is still in its infancy, scientists believe that the lack of eating is due to the color red, as it is associated with prohibition and danger.

Source: Benjamin Caro, "Color Psychology: Red Plates Make You Eat Less."
